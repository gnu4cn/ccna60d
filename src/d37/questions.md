8. What are the two flavours of ISDN?

10. `_______` functions by appending a label to any type of packet.
11. Frame Relay is based on which older protocol?
12. What are the three types of LMIs available?

15. Explain the difference between BECNs and FECNs.
16. PPP does not include data compression or error detection. True or false?
17. Name the PPP sublayers.
18. Write out the command to configure CHAP with PPP.

## 第 37 天答案

1. Circuit-switched, cell-switched, broadband, leased-line, and packet-switched.
2. Broadcast.
3. True.
4. Multipoint and Point-to-Point.
5. HDLC.
6. Metro Ethernet.
7. North America, Japan, and South Korea.
8. BRI and PRI.
9. ADSL.
10. MPLS.
11. The X.25 protocol.
12. CISCO, ANSI, and Q933a.
13. 10, sixth.
14. True.
15. Backward Explicit Congestion Notification (BECN): Frames in the direction opposite of the frame transmission experienced congestion; Forward Explicit Congestion Notification(FECN): Congestion was experienced in the direction of the frame transmission.
16. False.
17. NCP, LCP, and HDLC.
18. `ppp authentication chap`.
19. The show `interface serial [number]` command.
20. PAP, CHAP.
